    Competitions and Hackathons I've been to...

    IRIS, 2020

    Initiative for Research and Innovation in STEM (IRIS) promotes and nurtures science and scientific research among young Indian innovators. That can be used in hospitals to assist doctors and nurses in their work and provide medicine to patients.

    106th ISC, Jan 2019-20

    Rashtriya Kishore Vaigyanik Sammelan (Indian Science Congress), held in LPU, Jalandar Punjab inaugurated by Hon’ble Prime Minister Sri Narendra Modi on 3 January, 2019. I represented my innovation infront of the Prime Minister and other dignitaries. It was a 5 day long event, packed with interactive sessions with Nobel Laureates, exhibitions, competitions, and cultural events.

    JNNSMEE, 2019-20

    Jawaharlal Nehru National Science, Mathematics and Environment Exhibition (JNNSMEE) is an annual event held by NCERT to promote Science, Mathematics and Environment Education at the school level. I represented my innovation in the event and got the recognition from Hon'ble Minister Chief Minister of Gujrat, Sri Vijay Rupani. Times Of India (TOI) published my innovation in their newspaper.

    National Science Exhibition, CBSE

    The National Science Exhibition, organized annually by CBSE, serves as a platform to foster Science, Mathematics, and Environmental Education at the school level. During this event, I had the privilege of showcasing my innovative project. My work in the Medical Industry category received a special award. The innovation comprised two unique projects: the Med-Bot, designed to assist bed ridden patients in managing their medication schedule, and the Deaf Clock, a device tailored to set alarms specifically for the hearing-impaired community. These inventions aimed to improve the quality of life for individuals with different abilities.

    IRSC, Toyota Hackathon

    Indian Road Safety Campaign (IRSC) 2019 held in Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. It was a 36 hour long hackathon that aimed towards finding solutions to the problem related to Road Safety. I was the team leader, and represented my team to the dignitaries. We were among the top winners out of 100+ teams. We were awarded with a cash prize of Rs. 10,000/- and a certificate of appreciation. Our project solved the problems related to underage driving, drink and drive, theft protection, lending cars to unknows and many more. We also got a chance to present our project infront of the Hon'ble Minister of Road Transport and Highways, Sri Nitin Gadkari.
