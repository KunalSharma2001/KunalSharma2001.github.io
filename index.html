    106th ISC, Jan 2019-20

    JNNSMEE, 2019-20

    Jawaharlal Nehru National Science, Mathematics and Environment Exhibition (JNNSMEE) is an annual event held by NCERT to promote Science, Mathematics and Environment Education at the school level. I represented my innovation in the event and got the recognition from Hon'ble Minister Chief Minister of Gujrat, Sri Vijay Rupani. Times Of India (TOI) published my innovation in their newspaper.

    National Science Exhibition, CBSE

    The National Science Exhibition, organized annually by CBSE, serves as a platform to foster Science, Mathematics, and Environmental Education at the school level. During this event, I had the privilege of showcasing my innovative project. My work in the Medical Industry category received a special award. The innovation comprised two unique projects: the Med-Bot, designed to assist bed ridden patients in managing their medication schedule, and the Deaf Clock, a device tailored to set alarms specifically for the hearing-impaired community. These inventions aimed to improve the quality of life for individuals with different abilities.
